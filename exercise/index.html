Exercise Challenge

What is it?

This is the exercise page, maintained by Amit, for Amit, challenged by Amit.

A small, lump sized fat that started to show up with my belly. I decided to end it, and get fit. In 100 days.

General rules

  • Standing desk for
    • working
    • gaming
    • studying
    • movie
  • To bed within 1AM
  • Wake up and take shower in the morning
  • No working lying on bed in awkward position
  • 5 day exercise, 2 days break. Breaks are on Sunday and Tuesday. I take classes at varsity from 8AM to 9PM on those days, and more than 5000 steps are walked on both those days.
  • No matter what, maintain exercise

Exercise plan

First 30 days will be based on Foundation Light from Darebee.com.
